# Resume Setup

## 📄 Add Your Resume

The "Download Resume" button in the Hero section links to `/resume.pdf`.

### Steps to Add Your Resume:

1. **Export your resume as PDF**
    - Use Google Docs, Microsoft Word, or LaTeX
    - Ensure it's named exactly: `resume.pdf` (lowercase)

2. **Place it in the public folder**
    - Location: `/public/resume.pdf`
    - This makes it accessible at: `yoursite.com/resume.pdf`

3. **Alternative names** (if you prefer):
    - Update Hero.tsx line with your filename preference
    - Options: `Resume-YourName.pdf`, `CV.pdf`, etc.

### Resume Tips for Students:

- ✅ Include your education (GPA if 3.5+)
- ✅ List relevant coursework
- ✅ Showcase projects with tech stack
- ✅ Add GitHub and LinkedIn links
- ✅ Highlight any hackathons/competitions
- ✅ Keep it to 1 page (students)
- ✅ Use ATS-friendly format (avoid fancy graphics)
